Brown Sugar and Bacon Brussels Sprouts

Video Credit : Fresh Looks & Cooks

Uhhhh... Brussel Sprouts with brown sugar and bacon!?!?!? like what can go wrong? NOTHING!

Photo credit: thekitchn

Ingredients:

  • 5 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 2 tablespoons packed light or dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts
  • 3 slices thick-cut bacon (about 4 ounces)

Steps:

  1. Heat a large frying pan over medium heat. Meanwhile, place 3 tablespoons of the olive oil, 2 tablespoons packed brown sugar, 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt, and 1/2 teaspoon black pepper in a large bowl and stir to combine. Trim and halve 1 pound Brussels sprouts. Add the sprouts to the bowl and toss to combine. Dice 3 thick-cut bacon slices.

  2. Add the bacon and remaining 2 tablespoons olive oil to the pan. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the bacon is golden brown and crisp, 5 to 7 minutes. Using a slotted spoon, transfer the bacon to a paper towel-lined plate.

  3. Add the Brussels sprouts to the pan and arrange cut-side down in a single layer. Cook undisturbed until the cut-sides are dark golden brown, about 4 minutes. Return the bacon to the pan and toss to combine. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the sprouts are crisp tender, 3 to 4 minutes more.
